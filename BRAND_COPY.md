## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- **Warm & Playful** — Not corporate, not boring. Like talking to a cool friend.
- **Direct & Honest** — Say what we mean. No fluff.
- **Inclusive** — For EVERYONE. No gatekeeping.
- **Slightly Cheeky** — Personality matters.

### Writing Style
- **Short sentences.** Punchy. Direct.
- **Action-oriented.** Verbs first.
- **Use italics** for emphasis in headlines.
- **Emoji sparingly** in web copy (mainly social).
- **Avoid:** Corporate speak, buzzwords, over-explaining.

### Examples of Good Copy
✅ "Stick What You Feel." — Direct, action, clear.
✅ "Not just stickers. It's how you show up." — Emotional, identity-first.
✅ "Don't overthink it. Just stick it." — Relatable, removes friction.

### Examples of Bad Copy
❌ "Premium sticker solutions for creative professionals"
❌ "Elevate your aesthetic experience with our curated collections"
❌ "We specialize in bespoke vinyl decal customization"
